  mya - million years ago

So. We (humans)
   split from Laurasiatheria (incl. Carnivora) 95-85 mya,
   and from Dermoptera 79 mya.  By 62 mya, Glires (rabbits, rodents)
   split from the other placentals.  Unclear when the mrca
   of primates and glires lived -- but no earlier than 95 mya
   (Laurasiatheria-Euarchontoglires split).
   We split from New World Monkeys (Spider Monkey, others)
   only 35 mya.
